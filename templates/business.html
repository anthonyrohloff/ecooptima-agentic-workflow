Building Construction Sustainability

To incorporate plant bundles into LEED implementation, use the Sustainable Sites credit category, which supports landscape strategies such as native planting, stormwater management, and habitat restoration. LEED often aligns with the Sustainable SITES Initiative (SITES)—a complementary but separate rating system specifically focused on outdoor landscape design—offering additional guidance for integrating sustainable plant bundles.


Transportation

The building of urban trails offloads vehicles and provides linear green scape opportunities. Bundles can be planted along highways for the benefits as well as noise suppression. The 34-mile CROWN route is a local project for build a multiuse trail system.
